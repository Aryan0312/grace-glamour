About Grace & Glamour

A legacy of luxury, beauty, and trust — proudly serving the women of Surat since 2015.

Who We Are

Redefining Beauty in Surat

Grace & Glamour was founded by Sunita Shrivastav, a passionate makeup artist with a vision to create a luxurious sanctuary where every woman can discover her most beautiful, confident self.

What started as a single salon has grown into Surat's most trusted beauty destination with three branches in Jahangirpura, Rander, and Vesu. Specializing in Skin | Hair | Nail | Hair Extension | Make-Up, our highly trained artists bring international techniques and premium products together.

We believe every woman deserves to feel like royalty — on her wedding day, before an important event, or simply treating herself.

🎯

Our Mission

To empower every woman with the confidence that comes from looking and feeling her absolute best, delivered with warmth, professionalism, and quality.
